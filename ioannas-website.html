About me

I am a CS PhD candidate at GMU, working on cryptography with Prof. Foteini Baldimtsi. I got a BS degree in Applied Informatics from UOM, Greece in 2019 and a BS degree in Mathematics from AUTH, Greece in 2014. I was a research intern at IBM Research, Zurich for summer 2020 and a research intern at Visa Research, Palo Alto for summer 2022.
